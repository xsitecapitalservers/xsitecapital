How it works

Your retirement, by the numbers

Most retirement calculators assume stocks and bonds. This one is built for real estate. Enter your target retirement amount, timeline, and expected return — and we'll tell you exactly how much to invest annually in multifamily properties to get there.

1
Set your retirement year Drag the slider to your target retirement date. The calculator automatically sets your start year to today.
2
Enter your target amount How much do you want in your portfolio at retirement? Think about your desired lifestyle and annual income needs.
3
Adjust return & inflation assumptions XSITE's historical average is 29.97%. We default to a conservative 15% so your plan is built on solid ground.
4
See your annual investment goal We calculate the exact amount you need to invest each year, inflation-adjusted, with a growth chart to visualize the journey.
